About Sign2Text

Sign2Text is an advanced AI-powered system designed to bridge communication gaps for the deaf and hard-of-hearing community. By leveraging **computer vision** and **deep learning**, it translates **hand gestures into text**, making interactions more accessible and inclusive.

How It Works:

  • 📷 Captures live video of hand gestures.
  • 🖐 Extracts the **Region of Interest (ROI)** for accurate analysis.
  • 🤖 Processes the image through a trained **neural network**.
  • 🔤 Predicts the corresponding **text output**.
  • ✅ Enhances text accuracy with **intelligent word suggestions**.

"Empowering communication, one gesture at a time."

Sign2Text Team

🌍 Our Mission

We strive to **enhance accessibility** and **redefine communication** for individuals who rely on sign language. Our hope is that Sign2Text serves as a stepping stone towards a **more inclusive world** through the power of technology.
